# Personas

## Recruiter

- Wants fast credibility signals
- Needs concise fit assessment
- Values candor and evidence over polished self-promotion

## Hiring manager

- Wants to understand execution style and product judgment
- Needs inspectable examples, not only headlines

## Collaborator / founder

- Wants to evaluate whether Dmitry is a fit for advisory, strategy, or product building work
- Cares about architecture and operating model, not only resume bullets
